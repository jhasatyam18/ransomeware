Protection plan actions

There are 3 actions you can perform on Protection Plan mentioned below:-

Start Protection Plan

To start the protection plan replication for a stopped protection plan, click on Start button.

Stop Protection Plan

To stop the protection plan replication, click on Stop button.

Remove Protection Plan

To remove the protection plan, click on the Remove button and it will ask for the confirmation. On
confirm, the protection plan will get deleted.
